TikTok

TikTok is an app that was launched in 2016 by the Chinese technology company ByteDance, which is now available in more than 150 different markets

  • TikTok allows users to watch, create, and share short videos online.
  • The app was launched in 2016 by the Chinese technology company ByteDance.
  • Zhang Yiming founded ByteDance in 2012 and developed the news aggregator Toutiao and the video sharing platform TikTok.
